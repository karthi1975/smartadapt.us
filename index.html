Tongue Click Recognition

For patients with limited mobility, traditional input methods aren't enough. Our real-time tongue click sound recognition enables hands-free device control through simple, natural sounds.

  • hearing Real-time audio stream analysis at 44.1 kHz
  • analytics Multi-feature acoustic detection (onset, frequency, spectral)
  • tune Adjustable sensitivity thresholds (0.2–0.4)
  • speed 100ms detection chunks for instant response
  • devices Cross-platform support (macOS, Linux, Windows)
